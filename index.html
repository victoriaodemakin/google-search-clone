Lionel Messi - Wikipedia

Lionel Andrés Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Barcelona and the Argentina national team. Often considered one of the greatest players in the world and regarded by many as one of the greatest of all time, Messi has won a record six Ballon d'Or awards, and a joint-record three European Golden Shoes.
